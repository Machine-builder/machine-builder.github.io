Creepocalypse

The creepers are everywhere! Check out these brand new creeper types!



Have you looked at your house and thought to yourself "damn, I wish there were mobs that could blow this up and destroy all my hard work"? Well, in the very unlikely chance that you have, this addon is for you!

This addon adds in a truly immense amount (only 5 lol) new creepers to Minecraft Bedrock Edition.

The Creepers, In Order Of Appearance

Bonehead

The Bonehead is a skeleton-type creeper with a few surprises up its sleeves... If it had any sleeves. I won't spoil them for you though! Whack this creeper a couple times and you'll see what I'm talking about 👀
The stats are as follows:

  • ❤️ Health: 16HP, 8 × ❤️
  • 💥 Explosion Power: 3
  • 👞 Movement Speed: 0.2


Sporeburst

The Sporeburst is a fungal creeper that spawns on Mushroom Islands. This means mushroom islands are no longer safe from monsters.

  • Fun Fact - Sporebursts can be sheared for their mushroom hat, which can then be worn by players to blend in amongst other sporebursts!
The stats are as follows:
  • ❤️ Health: 24HP, 12 × ❤️
  • 💥 Explosion Power: 3
  • 👞 Movement Speed: 0.18


Timberling

The Timberling is a treelike creature, and can be found wandering the forests. Despite the peaceful appearance, they are still hostile, but they're also quite slow.
Upon exploding, Timberlings release a barrage of splinter projectiles in all directions.
The stats are as follows:

  • ❤️ Health: 30HP, 15 × ❤️
  • 💥 Explosion Power: 3
  • 👞 Movement Speed: 0.15


Sandstreak

The Sandstreak likes to hide itself amongst cactuses in deserts 🌵. You might not spot it right away, but if end up running by one you'll be sure to know when it jumps up and explodes in your face!
They're fast little buggers, and will explode a little faster than normal creepers too.
The stats are as follows:

  • ❤️ Health: 10HP, 5 × ❤️
  • 💥 Explosion Power: 2.5
  • ⏳ Fuse Time: 2.5s
  • 👞 Movement Speed: 0.3


Rampage

The Rampage is a terrifying and unstable creeper variant. If you see it, run.

  • Warning - The Rampage is extremely intense and unstable. If you see a Rampage and it sees you too, do NOT run to your house. It will approach your house and explode right through your walls, even if you're not within range.
The stats are as follows:
  • ❤️ Health: 24HP, 12 × ❤️
  • 💥 Explosion Power: 5 🔥
  • 👞 Movement Speed: 0.22
