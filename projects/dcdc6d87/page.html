More Creepers

You know how Minecraft sometimes feels too peaceful? Yeah, me too. This fixes that.
It won't be long before your entire house is blown up before your eyes with this truly life changing addon... We've got a wonderful addition of 6 new creepers for your Minecraft worlds.

Check out the release video of the addon for a proper walkthrough!


The Creepers You'll Find

Baby Creeper

Baby creepers spawn like passive animals, except they're very rare, so it'll be a challenge to get one as your pet ðŸ™‚
You can tame baby creepers using gunpowder!


Camoflauge Creeper

Camo creepers spawn in the same places as regular creepers, but are slightly rarer (And harder to spot!) They run faster than normal creepers, but have slightly less health. Explosion size is the same. They also camouflage depending on the block they're standing on (only on a set few blocks - 15 blocks in total)


Nether Creeper

Nether creepers spawn in, well, the nether. They have slightly more hp than normal creepers, and have the same explosion radius as normal creepers... but their explosions cause fire... They also drop gunpowder & blaze powder.


Ender Creeper

Ender creepers spawn in the end (& in the overworld rarely)

They run quite fast, and have 28 hp, so you better watch out


Fast Creeper

As the name suggests, fast creepers are fast. They also explode faster than regular creepers... Although they look charged, they have the same explosion radius as normal creepers. They spawn very rarely in the overworld.
